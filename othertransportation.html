Other forms of transportation

The island has many bike shops that guests can rent bicycles.

  • Single and tandem bikes
  • Maps to tourist destinations
  • Roadside assistance

Shop 2: 888-555-3333

  • Single bikes
  • Maps to tourist destinations
  • Will drop off bikes to the resort

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable.

Many of our guests prefer to walk through our accessible city streets and enjoy the beauty of Taniti city. Stop by the welcome center to get a complimentary walking tour map and guide.
